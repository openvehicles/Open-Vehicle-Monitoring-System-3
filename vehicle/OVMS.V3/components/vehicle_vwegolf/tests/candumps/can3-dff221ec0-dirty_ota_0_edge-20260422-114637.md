## Analysis (2026-04-22)

Sequence drove clima OFF/ON from the dashboard panel — and **no BAP
traffic appeared on KCAN for those events**. Zero `3T29 17332501` frames
in the entire 100s capture. The dash-panel commands reach the HVAC ECU
via LIN, not KCAN. This capture therefore cannot be used to decode the
clima start/stop BAP sequence — use `kcan-can3-clima_on_off.crtd` for
that (known-working OVMS-driven commands).

### Reusable finding: ECU periodic pushes on 0x17332501

The three "mystery" `3R29 17332501` frames at t≈8.8–9.6s are **ECU
self-broadcasts on its own request ID**, triggered by bus-awakening at
ignition-on, not client writes:

```
t=8.82s   19 41                          SF op=1 node=0x25 port=0x01
t=9.52s   90 04 19 59 28 00 00 04        MFS ch=1 op=1 port=0x19
t=9.59s   80 04 19 5a 28 02 00 04        MFS ch=0 op=1 port=0x1a
```

Matches the 4-cycle burst pattern documented in
`kcan-can3-clima_on_off.md` (section "Periodic status bursts on
0x17332501"), counter in `0x28–0x2F`. Not a command.
